About Me

Hello, my name is Gibson Green and I am currently a sophomore attending Florida Gulf Coast University. I am on schedule to graduate by the year 2021. I am a Software Engineering Major, and, on my free time, I am a Research Assistant on campus for Dr. Derek Buzasi. Currently, I am researching stellar bodies in the realms of AstroPhysics using data collected from the 2003 GALEX NASA mission. I am also an Honors College Mentor at FGCU. In summary, I mentor 5 new members of FGCU's Honors College, and get them acclimated to the Honors Community, as well as to the University. Finally, I am a brother in the Florida Xi Chapter of the Phi Delta Theta fraternity at FGCU.

Currently, in the world of Computer Science, I am just getting started along my path. Being a sophomore, I recently switch my Academic Major from Bioengineering (Pre-Med) with a minor in Computer Science, to switching completely over to Software Engineering. This being said, I cannot wait to begin this journey of my life and go down this new path. I find myself extremely intrigued and interested and look forward to my education!

In my free time I love watching TedTalks about anything having to do with Futurism, I play Intramural Basketball and follow anything that has to do with basketball, I love playing video games that include Fortnite, NBA 2k19, The Elder Scrolls series, etc.
